About Me

A little bit about me...

My name is Alexander Nieves and I enjoy coding. Since young I was always attracted to technology. In high school I took the Industrial Electronics workshop and thats where I was first introduced to computers. After high school I went to a technical school and took a course in Electronics Engineering. I graduated and found a job pretty quick. Around two years later I wanted to learn new skills so I decided to give computer programming a try. I was introduced to coding and thats when I knew that I wanted to code for a living.

After graduation I searched but I was never able get a job as a programmer. But I never got discouraged and continued to code because it was something I enjoyed. In the last few years I decided to try again to see if I can get a job coding. I started preparing on my own and decided to get some certifications. I received my Linux certifications through compTIA and then I wanted to give the Java certifications a try. I obtained the first two Java programming certifications from Oracle. Finally I decided to go for the Rutgers coding bootcamp to learn up to date technology. After graduation I started my own business as a freelance web developer: Codecipe LLC
